ABOUT OXFORD OMICLINE

Oxford Omicline is a bioinformatics and omics research consultancy dedicated to transforming complex biological data into meaningful, actionable insight. We work closely with academic institutions, biotechnology companies, and clinical researchers to deliver high-quality, transparent, and reproducible data analysis across genomics, transcriptomics, metabolomics, and integrative multi-omics workflows. Our approach combines scientific rigor, advanced computational methods, and clear biological interpretation to support publication-ready research, grant applications, and data-driven decision making

.

Our Services

Single-cell RNA-seq Analysis

We provide end-to-end single-cell RNA sequencing analysis, including quality control, normalization, clustering, differential expression, and biological interpretation.

Spatial Transcriptomics Analysis

Advanced spatial omics workflows combining spatial information with transcriptomic profiles to understand tissue organization and cellular context.

Bulk RNA-seq & Differential Expression

Robust processing, alignment, quantification, and differential expression analysis for bulk RNA-seq experiments with publication-ready outputs.

Multi-Omics Integration

Integration of genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and other omics datasets to uncover complex biological insights.

Reproducible, Publication-Ready Workflows

Transparent, well-documented pipelines designed to ensure reproducibility, traceability, and confidence in published results.

Biological Interpretation & Reporting

Clear, biologically meaningful reports with figures, pathway analysis, and actionable insights tailored to your research goals.

Meet the Team
